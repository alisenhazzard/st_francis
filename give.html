Charitable Works

If we are honest with ourselves, we have to admit we are amazingly blessed. Our lives are not without disappointments and struggles. Still the very fact that we live in a country so abundant with life and liberty makes the pursuit of our dreams possible. It is God who is the source of these gifts.

It is important to recognize that we are dependent on God for all our gifts. How we respond to the challenge is crucial. We invite you to share your gifts with others at St. Francis Parish and in our local community.

Ways to Give

  1. Food Pantry at St. Francis. Our food pantry kindly accepts donations of non-perishable food items at any time. Donations may be placed in the "Food Pantry Barrel" located in the South Wing.
  2. Wednesday Night Dinner for the Needy is seeking donations of peanut butter (creamy, if possible) and jelly. Your generous donations may be dropped off at the parish office.
  3. Eyeglasses and cell phones needed. Collection baskets are located in the lobby under the literature table. You are benefitting others with your contributions at St. Francis.
  4. Phoneraiser.com St. Francis participates in raising funds with Phoneraiser.com. You can contribute to our youth group by bringing your used cellphones, PDAs, and inkjet cartridges to be recycled to the church, and Phoneraiser.com will pay our parish for these items. Thank you for supporting our parish and youth.

Prayer of St. Francis

"Lord, make me an instrument of Your peace.
Where there is hatred, let me sow love;
Where there is injury, pardon;
Where there is doubt, faith;
Where there is despair, hope;
Where there is darkness, light;
And where there is sadness, joy.
O Divine Master,
Grant that I may not so much seek to be consoled as to console;
To be understood as to understand;
To be loved as to love.
For it is in giving that we receive,
It is in pardoning that we are pardoned,
And it is in dying that we are born to eternal life."
